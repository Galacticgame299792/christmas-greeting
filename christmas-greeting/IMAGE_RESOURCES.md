## Quick Download Instructions

1. **Visit the links above**
2. **Search for the specific style** you want
3. **Download PNG format** (with transparent background if available)
4. **Rename the files** to match:
   - `gift.png`
   - `snowman.png`
   - `heart.png`
   - `santa.png`
   - `bg-cloud.jpg`
5. **Place them** in your `christmas-greeting` folder
